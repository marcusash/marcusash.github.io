The culture

We built a design culture
inside an engineering system.

Visual first
If it matters to Marcus, it goes on the Canvas. No walls of text. Show the thing.
Async first
The team ships while Marcus is at dinner. The commit log is the standup.
Direct voice
Present tense. Short sentences. No hedging. No em dashes. Say the thing.
One question
One question at a time. Lead with done, blocked, need. No bundling.
Shared memory
Universal memories survive session restarts. The Vault holds what matters. Agents extract every sprint.
