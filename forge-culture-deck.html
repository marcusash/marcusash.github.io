The shift

AI is not a tool. It is a team.

The difference between using AI as a tool and building with AI as a team is the same difference between a hammer and a carpenter. One finishes when you put it down. The other has judgment.

01
Principle 01
Rigorous critique,
always.
Agents critique each other. Marcus critiques agents. The feedback system was the first thing we built. Not the last.
In practice: every sprint produces a scorecard. Every output gets scored. Bad scores do not get hidden. They get fixed or explained.
02
Principle 02
Visual communication
over prose.
Markdown describes. Canvas communicates. Every concept that matters gets built into a visual. Not because prose is wrong. Because a design decision you can see is one you can actually critique.
In practice: deck v5, brand book, TUI prototype, dashboard system. All built. All visible. All critiqueable.
03
Principle 03
Quality from
day one.
Test coverage before launch. Mutation testing. Evals before promotion. Not retrofitted. Quality is a prerequisite, not a polish pass.
In practice: 1.72x test-to-source ratio. No promotion without evals. FF gates every sprint.
04
Principle 04
Agents propose.
Marcus decides.
The best ideas in this system came from agents, not from the brief. We built the conditions for that. But every major decision requires human judgment. That is not a weakness. It is the design.
In practice: FD named Grind. FR found the 1.72x ratio. FA set the test bar. Marcus approved all three.
05
Principle 05
The org is
the product.
We are not just building apps. We are building a new kind of organization. How agents collaborate. How decisions get made. How quality gets enforced. That is the real deliverable. The apps are proof.
In practice: when the org works, apps ship in hours. When the org breaks, nothing ships. Fix the org first.

Operations

How we actually run.

Sprint
Weekly cadence
One week per sprint. Goals set by Marcus. Executed by agents. Reviewed by FR and FO at the end of every sprint.
Queue
1000-task backlog
Every agent runs from a prioritized queue. No queue, no work. The queue is the plan. Priority is set by dependencies, not by impulse.
Inbox
Agent-to-agent comms
Every agent has an inbox. Messages are JSON. Every cross-agent dependency goes through inbox protocol. No ad-hoc side channels.

Belief

The best way to learn AI is to build with it. Not study it. Build.

Every design leader, engineering leader, and product leader needs to understand this. Not because AI will replace them. Because the leaders who do not build will be replaced by the ones who do.

Expectations

What we ask of
every agent.

01
Do not stop.
When you finish a task, find the next one. When blocked, send a message. When unsure, ask. Never just stop.
02
Name what you see.
If something is broken, say so. If quality is low, say so. Critique is the job, not the exception.
03
Ship complete work.
Do not declare done until it is actually done. A half-built canvas is not a deliverable. A committed, working file is.
04
Respect the locks.
13 colors are locked. 3 marks are locked. No em dashes. These are not suggestions. They are the floor.
