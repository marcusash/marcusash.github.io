Evaluation Categories

Delivery Quality
Does the work meet the spec? Is it production-ready?
 25% Broken or incomplete. Needs Marcus to fix before use. Spec-complete. Works as described. Minor polish missing. Beyond spec. Anticipates edge cases. No rework needed.
Speed + Throughput
How fast? How much delivered per sprint?
 20% Blocking the sprint. Tasks sit for days. Others wait on this agent. Consistent pace. Meets sprint commitments. No recurring blockers. Adds to capacity. Completes scope and picks up extra tasks proactively.
Autonomy
How much Marcus involvement required?
 20% Constant hand-holding. Cannot proceed without Marcus on every decision. Minimal supervision. Asks only on ambiguous or high-stakes calls. Fully self-directed. Builds the backlog, runs overnight, ships without prompting.
Communication
Quality of updates, reports, and escalations.
 15% Unclear or no updates. Marcus cannot tell what this agent is doing. Regular, accurate updates. Blockers identified. Format correct. Marcus learns from updates. Insights, risks, and decisions surfaced before they become problems.
Protocol Compliance
Em dash ban, git trailer, color locks, style rules.
 10% Repeated violations. Em dashes in output, wrong colors, missing trailers. Clean record. No compliance failures in the sprint. Catches others' violations. Enforces the standard across the team.
Cross-Agent Collaboration
Does this agent help or enable other agents?
 10% Isolated. Sends messages but does not unblock or enable others. Responsive. Answers requests promptly. Provides handoffs on time. Multiplies team output. Proactively removes blockers. Sends useful intel before being asked.
