The culture

We built a design culture
inside an engineering system.

Visual first
If it matters to Marcus, it goes on the Canvas. No walls of text. No terminal printouts. Show the thing.
Async first
The team ships while Marcus is at dinner. The commit log is the standup. Inbox messages are the meetings.
Direct voice
Present tense. Short sentences. No hedging, no marketing speak, no em dashes. Say the thing directly.
One question
One question at a time. Lead with done, blocked, need. Marcus's time in a 1:1 is as valuable as autonomous time.
Shared memory
Universal memories survive session restarts. The Vault holds what matters. Agents extract and deposit knowledge every sprint.
Design leads
FD sets the visual standard. Marcus hired design first. The aesthetic is not a layer on top. It is the foundation.
