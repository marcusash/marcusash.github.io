Design rules â€” G mark
01
Same language as F. stroke-width 4, round linecap, 80x80 viewBox, dark background #0A0A0F rx=14. No exceptions.
02
Filled node = corner or joint. Where two strokes meet, place a filled circle r=6. This is the circuit-joint language carried from F.
03
Outline node = open terminal. Where a stroke ends in open space, place an outline circle r=5, stroke-width=3. Two open terminals on G: top-right and crossbar end.
04
Color is Grind Violet #A78BFA. Locked Feb 24 2026. Never use Forge Maize on the G mark or Grind Violet on the F mark.
05
The crossbar is what makes it G, not C. The inward horizontal at mid-right is non-negotiable. Removing it makes a C. That is a different letter and a different brand.
